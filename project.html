Spy Project

For this project will be building a generic website that will seem to ask mundane question to normal users, but you secretly are looking for a spy! anyone visiting your website will be asked a series of question, only the spy will be able to give specific answers you expect. If all questions are answered correctly, you will post a secret message in console for the spy to read!

Here are the four consitions you will check for the spy:

  • The Spy has the same first letter of her First Name and Last Name
  • The Spy is between the age of 20 and 30
  • The Spy is at least 170 centimeters tall
  • The Spy has a pet name that ends with the letter "y".

A Spy with the following information should see you secret message

  • Jose Johnson
  • 26 years old
  • 175 cm tall
  • Pet name is "Sammy"
